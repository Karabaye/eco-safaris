About Eco-Safaris Rwanda

Eco-safaris Rwanda is the Rwandan registered tourism company in Rwanda development Board which is based in Kigali and the member of Tour operators, Rwanda Tours and Travel association(RTTA)

Its commitment is to protect environment and community based tourism for sustainable tourism.

Eco-safaris Rwanda organizes and arranges eco-safaris and ground tour operator to East African and sub-Saharan African countries (i.e. South Africa, Botswana,Zambia,Zimbabwe and Namibia) .

Eco-safaris Rwanda selects/ has the first class vehicles for transporting their clients depending on the number of them and the main objective of the safari. It has modified 4WDs (4x4) with an open roof to maximize your game viewing and photography.

Eco-safaris Rwanda is an Africa travel specialist company due to have the professional English and French speaking guides/ driver guides ,Eco-Safaris Rwanda also connects you to the Arabic,Italian,germany,Spanish and Japanese speaking guides and have knowledge of all sites.

CANCELATION POLICY

Should you need to cancel a booking, please notify Eco-Safaris Rwanda immediately, in writing? A cancelation charge will apply from the date of receipt of the notification. Please make sure you receive acknowledgement of your cancelation.

Cancelation fees are as follows

More than 30 days prior to arrival: 25% of the total amount due

Less than 30 days prior to arrival: 50% of the total amount due

Less than 7 days prior to arrival: 100% of the total amount due
